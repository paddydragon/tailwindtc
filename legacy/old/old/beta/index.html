Providing children the wings to take flight

Tailwind is a competitive youth running club, based in Westchester, NY, targeting boys and girls (ages 6-18) from beginner to elite. Tailwind offers a structured training program which includes strength and conditioning, track running, and group trail runs in local parks and preserves. Our core group of athletes have been training together for over four years and are eager to greet new children who share their passion for running and having fun. Although the program targets youth athletes of all abilities, many of our athletes have achieved great success on regional, national, and global levels.
