Tailwind's Coaches

Carl Curran, founder and Head Coach

Coach Curran has been a competitive runner and triathlete for over 19 years. He has completed 18 marathons (including qualifying for and completing six Boston Marathons), seven Ironman distance triathlons (including qualifying for and completing the Ironman World Championships in Kona, HI) and over 100 running and triathlon races of shorter distances.

Carl has been coaching youth athletics for over 18 years beginning with baseball and softball and has been coaching youth triathlon and running for over nine years. Coach Curran is a Certified USATF Coach. His athletes have achieved outstanding results at local, national and international levels.



Fatlume Bakraqi, Assistant Coach

Fatlume Bakraqi has been running competitively for 15 years, and was a 10-year champion for the 800m and 1500m in Kosovo. She has many championship titles in former Yugoslavia. She has run the New York and Chicago Marathons and is now training for the upcoming Boston Marathon. She has been an Assistant Coach since 2015, and uses her personal experience to help improve the young athletes of Tailwind.



Deborah Young, PT, DPT, physical therapist

Deborah Young ran her first race on the track at 2 1/2 years old and went on to compete with a youth track program very similar to Tailwind Track Club. She attended Williams College, where she ran cross country, indoor, and outdoor track, serving as team captain and traveling to NCAA Div III National Championships in both cross country (5k) and outdoor track (10k) multiple times. She earned her Doctorate of Physical Therapy at the MGH Institute of Health Professions in Boston, MA. She currently practices as a pediatric physical therapist and is the mother of three children. She continues to enjoy the challenge of training and, most recently, has focused on the marathon and half-marathon distances.
