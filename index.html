Providing children the wings to take flight

Tailwind is a competitive youth running club, based in Westchester, NY, supporting boys and girls (ages 6-18) from beginner to elite. Tailwind offers a structured training program which includes strength and conditioning, track running, and group trail runs in beautiful local parks and preserves. Many of our athletes have been training together for years and are always eager to greet new children who share their passion for running and having fun. We regularly work with children "finding their legs" for the first time, though many of our athletes have achieved great success on regional, national, and global levels.
